    Cereal Production

    The vizulaization below shows the amount of cereal production in different countries over the years from 1961 to 2021

    What are some important points to notice in the image?

    - We can see that over the years United States, China, and India are the greatest producers of crops

    - The continent of Africa produces the least amount of crops that get turned into Cereal

    - Russia's data doesn't start till 1992 which is due to the the dissolution of the USSR, which in turn opened up Russia to be able to share their data globally

    - Over the years India's cereal crop production increased to be on par with the United States and China by the year 2022

    - The countries with the highest amount of populations produce the most amount of cereal crops this can be seen with United States, India, and China
